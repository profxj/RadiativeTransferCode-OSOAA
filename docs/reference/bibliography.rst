Primary OSOAA Reference
-----------------------

Main OSOAA Paper
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. [Chami2015] Chami M, Lafrance B, Fougnie B, Chowdhary J, Harmel T, Waquet F (2015).
   OSOAA: a vector radiative transfer model of coupled atmosphere-ocean system for a rough sea surface 
   application to the estimates of the directional variations of the water leaving reflectance to better 
   process multi-angular satellite sensors data over the ocean.
   *Optics Express* 23(21): 27829-27852.
   https://doi.org/10.1364/OE.23.027829

   **Description:** The definitive reference for OSOAA, describing the model formulation, successive orders 
   method, surface interface treatment, and validation.

Citation Guidelines
-------------------

When using OSOAA in publications, please cite:

**Minimum citation:**

Chami et al. (2015) [Chami2015]_

**For specific features:**

- **Surface model:** Cox & Munk (1954) [CoxMunk1954]_
- **Bio-optical model:** Morel (1988) [Morel1988]_
- **Aerosol models:** Shettle & Fenn (1979) [Shettle1979]_ or d'Almeida et al. (1991) [dAlmeida1991]_

**Example citation:**

   "Radiative transfer simulations were performed using OSOAA v2.0 (Chami et al., 2015), 
   a vector radiative transfer model for coupled atmosphere-ocean systems. The model employs 
   the successive orders of scattering method with a Cox-Munk rough surface (Cox and Munk, 1954) 
   and bio-optical parameterization based on Morel (1988)."
